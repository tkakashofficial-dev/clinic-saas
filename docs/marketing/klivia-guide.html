How it works

A normal day at your clinic

Here's how a patient flows through Klivia from the front desk to the pharmacy — the same steps your team will do every day.

1

A patient walks in Reception

Reception opens Patients → Register and enters a name and phone — that's enough. The patient gets a clinic number (like P-000482) automatically.

2

Book the visit Reception

In Appointments → Book, reception searches the patient, picks the doctor and the time. Done in seconds.

3

Patient arrives → Check in Reception

Press Check in. The doctor instantly sees the patient in their waiting room and gets a notification. No shouting across the clinic.

4

The consultation Doctor

The doctor opens the waiting patient and records the diagnosis, vitals and treatment. Need a form? Print the pre-filled dental or general intake sheet for the patient to sign.

5

Prescription in one tap Doctor

Add medicines — names are suggested from your own pharmacy, and quick chips fill the dose pattern (1-0-1) and timing (after food). Out comes a beautiful branded PDF to print or share.

6

Pharmacy & the big picture Admin

Stock is tracked in Inventory, with low-stock and expiry alerts. The Dashboard and Reports show the day, the month, and how each doctor is doing — no counting by hand.

Everyone sees only what they need

Admins run the clinic, doctors see their patients and consultations, reception handles the front desk. One person can be more than one role — a doctor who owns the clinic is simply Admin + Doctor.

Questions clinics ask us

Is my patient data safe?
Yes. Each clinic's data is isolated at the core — it's architecturally impossible for another clinic to see your records. Passwords are stored only as secure one-way hashes, and we never sell your data or use it for advertising.
Can we move from paper or another system?
Absolutely — you can start fresh in minutes, and on the Growth plan we help migrate your existing patient records.
The clinic owner is not a doctor. Does that work?
Yes. Ownership and being a doctor are separate. An owner runs the clinic; only people with the Doctor role appear in the booking list.
What if we want to stop? Cancel any time, no lock-in, and export your data — your records belong to you.
