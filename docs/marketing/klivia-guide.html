02

Patients

Register in 20 seconds · every record one search away

What it does

  • Register with just a name and phone — details can come later
  • Automatic clinic numbers (P-000482) like your paper files
  • Instant search by name or phone
  • Full patient record: contacts, medical conditions, visit timeline
  • Every past diagnosis, vitals and prescription in one scroll
  • Edit details any time — reception typos are a daily reality
  • Printable intake forms: 🦷 dental & 🩺 general, pre-filled with the patient's details and your clinic letterhead

How your team uses it

  1. New patient walks in → Patients → Register → name + phone → Save
  2. Returning patient? Type three letters of their name — found
  3. Click the patient → their whole history opens
  4. Print the intake form; the patient ticks the medical checklist and signs consent in the waiting room; the doctor fills the clinical sections during examination

03

Appointments & waiting room

Book · check in · treat — the heartbeat of the clinic

What it does

  • Book in seconds: search patient, pick doctor, pick time
  • Day navigator with a beautiful calendar — jump to any date
  • Live statuses: Scheduled → Waiting → In progress → Completed
  • One-tap Check in — the doctor instantly sees who's waiting
  • Filter by doctor or status; "My patients only" for doctors
  • Automatic reminders for tomorrow's appointments

How your team uses it

  1. Reception books the visit while the patient is still on the phone
  2. Patient arrives → press Check in → doctor gets a notification
  3. Doctor opens the waiting patient and starts the consultation
  4. Status flips to Completed automatically when the visit is saved
04

Consultations & prescriptions

Diagnosis, vitals and a designer PDF in one tap

What it does

  • Record diagnosis, treatment notes and vitals (BP, pulse, temp, weight)
  • Medicine names auto-suggested from your own pharmacy stock
  • One-tap dose chips the Indian way: 1-0-0, 1-0-1, 1-1-1, 0-0-1
  • Timing chips: after food, before food, at bedtime, with warm water
  • Beautiful branded PDF prescription — your clinic name, letterhead and doctor's signature line
  • Every consultation lands in the patient's history automatically

How your team uses it

  1. Doctor taps the waiting patient → Consult
  2. Types the diagnosis; vitals take ten seconds
  3. Adds medicines — three taps per line with the chips
  4. Save & complete → prescription PDF is ready to print or share

Why the PDF matters

The prescription is the paper your patient carries to the pharmacy and keeps at home — it's your clinic's business card. Klivia's prescriptions look like they came from a premium hospital, with your name on top.

05

Pharmacy & inventory

Never run out mid-treatment again

What it does

  • Track medicines, consumables (gloves, cartridges) and equipment
  • Each item: unit, price, stock count, reorder level, expiry date
  • Low stock rises to the top — with a red chip you can't miss
  • Expiring-soon warnings (60 days ahead) so nothing is wasted
  • ± Stock in/out with a tap — counts can never go below zero
  • Prescriptions suggest medicine names from this same shelf, so spelling always matches what you actually stock
  • Doctors can see stock too — they never prescribe what you don't have

How your team uses it

  1. Add your medicines once: name, unit, opening stock, reorder level
  2. Supplier delivers? ＋ Stock in with the quantity
  3. Dispense or use stock? − Stock out
  4. Every morning, the top of the list IS your purchase list
